Frequently Asked Questions

What room sharing options are available at Kanha Kutir?

We offer comfortable single, double, and triple sharing rooms. Each room comes equipped with individual beds, personal wardrobes, and dedicated study desks.

What are the main amenities included in the monthly rent?

Our all-inclusive package includes high-speed Wi-Fi,24*7 hot and cold water,common RO drinking water,common pantry facility,common refrigerator, common washing machines for laundry.

What safety and security measures are in place?

Student safety is our top priority. The premises are under 24/7 CCTV surveillance, we have a secure gate entry and strict register entry systems.

Is there any extra charge for electricity?

No! There is no extra charge for electricity, it is iclusive in the rent

Are meals provided and included?

No! There is no meal inclusion in the rent, however you can be provided with various tiffin centre contact numbers for your convenience but its all tenant and the meal provider responsibility, we do not take any responsibility related to meals.

Is night shift allowed?

Primarily no but if the tenant is found trustworthy and reliable it could be allowed with proper verification and under strict rules and guidelines.

Are working women allowed?

Absolutely! We are open for both students and working professionals.

AC or Non AC?

Each and every room is equipped with all premium facilities including the AC

What are the hostel timings and curfew?

To ensure student safety, the main gate closes at 11:00 PM. Any late entries require prior written permission or an alert from parents or guardians.
